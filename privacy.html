Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

1. System Data Overview

Metal Forge operates as a high-velocity utility for prompt generation and session management. This policy outlines how your data is handled.

2. Information Processing

  • User Inputs: Data entered into style/theme fields is processed securely for inference.
  • Session Artifacts: Generated outputs are stored via secure cloud protocols linked to your session.

3. Security

We leverage Vercel Edge Gateways and encrypted protocols to ensure your interaction with generative AI models remains isolated and protected.

4. Data Deletion & Contact

You retain full control over your session. To delete your data, you may clear your browser cache/app storage or simply uninstall the application, which severs your unique session token and orphans all associated cloud-cached data.

5. Third-Party Services

Metal Forge utilizes standard cloud infrastructure to power AI-generation pipelines, including Vercel for hosting, Google Firebase for session persistence, and Google Gemini API for prompt processing.
